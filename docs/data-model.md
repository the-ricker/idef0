# Data model

An IDEF diagram will have

1. 1 or more activities
1. 0 or more concepts (also called ICOM)
1. Concepts are:
	1. inputs
	1. outputs
	1. mechanisms
	1. controls
1. Inputs enter the left side of an activity
1. Controls enter the top side of an activity
1. Mechanisms enter the bottom side of an activity
1. Outputs exit the right side of an activity
1. An activity will have 
	1. zero or more inputs
	1. zero or more outputs
	1. zero or more mechanisms
	1. zero or more controls
1. All concepts come from either 
	1. external or 
	1. as the output of an activity
